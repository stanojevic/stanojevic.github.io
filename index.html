Hello

My name is Miloš and I'm a Senior Research Scientist at DeepMind working on language processing.

My research interests lie in the area of structure prediction using deep neural models. The main direction I'm pursuing at the moment is in incremental syntactic parsing with rich grammar formalisms such as CCG and Minimalist Grammars. I do this research both in the context of computational linguistics (designing cognitively plausible parsers) and natural language processing (for instance, building machine translation systems).
