Hello

My name is Miloš and I'm a Post-Doc at the University of Edinburgh working with Prof. Mark Steedman.

My research interests lie mostly in the area of incremental syntactic parsing with rich grammar formalisms such as CCG and Minimalist Grammars. I do this research both in the context of computational linguistics (designing cognitively plausible parsers) and natural language processing (for instance, building machine translation systems).
